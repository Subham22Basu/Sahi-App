Data rich.
Financially invisible.

Translating complex backend architecture into a fluid UI.

The Data Haven.

UX Goal: Establish Trust & Stability.
We visualize the direct connection to Account Aggregators. Sahi acts as an automated escrow, analyzing bank statements to buffer high-earning festival weeks and release funds during slow periods—creating a synthetic, stable salary.

UX Goal: Emphasize Portability.
Workers link their identities via Aadhaar and DigiLocker. Sahi routes these verified credentials instantly, allowing health benefits and micro-pension plans (NPS) to remain active and tethered to the individual, regardless of which app they drive for.

UX Goal: Drive Financial Action.
We gamify real-time UPI network history and platform payout regularity. Sahi translates this daily cash flow into a high-trust credibility rating, connecting users to lenders via the OCEN network for instant, low-interest equipment loans.

04 / Systems Thinking

India Stack Integration.

Sahi does not rely on screen-scraping or manual uploads. The product acts as an orchestration layer, securely plugging into India's highly advanced Digital Public Infrastructure (DPI) to authenticate and assess workers in real-time.

1

Identity & Labor

UIDAI & e-Shram

Sahi utilizes Aadhaar e-KYC for instant onboarding. We cross-reference this with the Ministry of Labour’s e-Shram portal to formally classify the user as an unorganized sector worker, immediately qualifying them for state-sponsored subsidies and PMJAY health top-ups.

2

Financial Flow

Account Aggregators (AA)

Operating within the RBI's AA framework, Sahi requests encrypted, user-consented access to historical bank statements. Our backend parses transaction metadata to identify algorithmic payouts from platforms (Uber, Swiggy) to establish true earning capacity without tax returns.

3

Real-Time Intent

UPI Network Data

We monitor the frequency, velocity, and success rates of UPI transactions. Consistent, small-value transfers (buying fuel daily, paying local vendors) are fed into our Intent Signal Algorithm, predicting a borrower's likelihood to repay micro-installments.

4

Capital Access

OCEN Integration

Sahi functions as a Loan Service Provider (LSP) on the Open Credit Enablement Network. We package the aggregated Trust Metric and present it to participating NBFCs, allowing lenders to underwrite and disburse tailored sachet-loans (₹5k - ₹20k) in minutes.

05 / Security Strategy

Privacy by Design.

Handling sensitive Aadhaar and banking data requires military-grade architecture. Sahi is engineered to act as a router, not a honeypot.

Explicit Consent Architecture

Built strictly on the Data Empowerment and Protection Architecture (DEPA). Users grant granular, time-bound consent for every data pull via the Account Aggregator network. Sahi cannot access data invisibly.

Zero-Knowledge Storage

Raw transaction logs are analyzed on edge or instantly discarded. Sahi stores only the computed "Trust Metric" hashes, ensuring that even in the event of a breach, raw financial histories are mathematically inaccessible.

Data Tokenization

When communicating with third-party lenders via OCEN, Aadhaar numbers and bank account details are fully tokenized. Lenders receive an encrypted intent profile, protecting the worker from targeted predatory lending.

Data Locality Compliance

Adhering strictly to RBI circulars, all transactional data processing and cloud hosting is maintained on localized, Indian-based AWS servers (ap-south-1), ensuring full regulatory compliance.

06 / Viability

The Business Model.

To ensure long-term sustainability and keep the core platform free for gig workers, Sahi operates on a hybrid B2B2C monetization structure.

A

LSP Commissions (Lending)

By acting as an LSP on the OCEN protocol, Sahi earns a 1.5% - 2.5% origination fee from partnered Banks and NBFCs for every successfully disbursed micro-loan, solving the high Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC) for lenders.

B

Platform Partnership SaaS

Aggregators (Zomato, Urban Company) pay a monthly subscription fee per active user to integrate Sahi as a "worker retention and wellness benefit." This reduces their churn rates, which currently exceed 15% monthly.

C

Premium API Services

While the core app is free, Sahi charges a nominal fee for advanced financial actions, such as automated multi-app tax filing (ITR-4 for presumptive taxation) and custom micro-insurance claim routing.

07 / Scalability

Future Prospects.

The gig economy is just the wedge. Sahi is positioned to become the definitive financial layer for the broader creator and informal economy.

ONDC Integration (Micro-Merchants)

As the Open Network for Digital Commerce (ONDC) expands, Sahi will adapt its Trust Metric for independent online sellers. By underwriting store inventory based on ONDC buyer transaction data rather than CIBIL scores, Sahi can finance millions of home-based D2C businesses.

AI-Driven Income Smoothing

Transitioning the Data Haven from a reactive savings buffer to a predictive AI engine. By analyzing macroeconomic data, local festival calendars, and weather patterns (which affect delivery demand), Sahi will accurately predict a worker's upcoming slow week and preemptively adjust their auto-save quotas.

Peer-to-Peer Mutual Aid Pools

Leveraging the network effect to create verified, community-driven emergency funds (modernized Chit Funds). Workers within the same high-tier Trust Metric bracket can pool micro-savings daily, generating low-interest, community-approved emergency capital instantly.
